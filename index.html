A lemmings-like game with incredible
Minions in insidious situations.

"Minions within a Dungeon" is a comprehensive gameplay mix made of classics and aligned to a lemmings-like gameplay style. As out of nowhere you will gain control over the Minions as the overlord "Stubborn Helmet" and guide them through insidious situations. Always with a bit of a subliminal joke in your controversy Story.

Getting through the game requires you to place support-items strategically, prevent minions from falling the cliffs, self-imposed explosion and even resurrect them from the death!
