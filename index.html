About minions within a dungeon

'Minions within a dungeon' is a 2D lemmingslike indie game which combines classic gameplay with new mechanics and an unique graphicstyle.

You know those good old days were you actually saw a pixel and it was like about half an inch? Probably the first game I played in my life was lemmings via DOS.

So this is were we start. The traditional dungeon guiding experience of a lemmingslike game combined with modern mechanics. You guide your minions through several traps besides enemies and solving puzzles on your way.

Everything is set up in an modern beautiful art style, strange humor and always with a secret behind the corner.

You are an overlord helping out your minions by placing items all over the dungeons. Some items can be picked up. Some are used directly, others enhancing abilities. There are even some you can't place but collect in levels or harvest from a plant! Each item enables one or more new mechanics.

So if you merge it all together you got a: lemmingslike puzzle 2D platformer mechanics tactics magament game. Hell, it's just a game: If you are interested follow the development or ask me directly on twitter: @sam_vieten.
