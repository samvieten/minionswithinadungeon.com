Frequently asked questions

What is the gameplay about?

The gameplay is a lemmingslike with tons of new mechanics. You will control your minions in an indirect way by supporting them with items. Each item has one or more abilites which affects the environment and minions. The focus in development is heavily relying on indepth polished gameplay mechanics.

On which platforms will MwaD be released?

Minions within a dungeon will be available on Linux, Mac and Windows via several services like steam and itch. There will be an official announcement for that but be shure - your dektop platform is covered.

Pressbuild & early access?

As in this state of development there are no prebuild offerings yet. But as the game got in the last development phase, a pressbuild will be available. As for early access, there will be a "early bird" version offering a lower price for early bird and prepurchases. Those versions will be available only a few weeks before official release (1.0).

Which Engine and tools are being used?

100% independend: The game is written in javascript using an compiler to run it on every platform. There are no big brands used to finish minions within a dungeon. Tools are being used: PhaserJS, Pixi.js, nwjs, nodejs, affinity designer, simple garage band.

Who is developing MwaD?

One person: developing, designing, sound engeneering: you can follow me at twitter: @sam_vieten.

When will it be released?

As for indie-developers this is an aweful question since development heavily relies on spare time. The game is targeted to be finished in 2017. I will not make any announcements or promises yet. There will be an official announcement.

What will it cost?

Hopefully enough that the work can be conitnued for free expansions. The price target will be at the lower point of video games were other indie-titles are positioned.
